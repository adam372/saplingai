Privacy Policy

Sapling AI is built for students. That means your data should stay yours. This page explains what we collect, why we collect it, and what you control.

What We Collect

Sapling AI may collect the minimum information needed to run the app and improve it:

Account Info

If you create an account, we may store basic details such as your email and authentication identifiers.

App Content

Things you add in the app (like tasks, plans, or study materials) may be stored so they sync across your devices.

Usage Data

Basic analytics (like feature usage and crashes) may be collected to improve stability and performance.

How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to: provide core features (planning, revisions, syncing), improve app performance, and support users. We do not sell your personal data.

AI Features

If you use AI features, the text you submit may be processed to generate responses. Avoid sharing sensitive personal information in AI prompts.

Sharing

We only share data with service providers needed to operate the app (such as hosting, analytics, or authentication providers). These providers are required to protect your data.

Your Choices

You can:

Access & Update

Update your account details and the content you store in the app.

Delete Content

Remove tasks or materials you’ve added whenever you want.

Request Deletion

Contact support to request account deletion where applicable.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, contact us via the Support page.
